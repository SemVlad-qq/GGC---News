PlayStation Plus Free Games For July 2024 Are Live Now

PlayStation has released this month's batch of free games for PlayStation Plus subscribers: Borderlands 3, NHL 24, and Among Us. A fun sequel to Gearbox's excellent shoot-and-loot games, the story of Borderlands 3 revolves around a cult known as The Children of the Vault, billions of shiny weapons, and family drama. While the main storyline isn't the best, the four DLC expansions tell a much better story and one of them sort of sees Handsome Jack return to menace players. The timing on this addition to PS Plus is also pretty good, as the live-action Borderlands movie will hit theaters in August.Next on the list is EA Sports NHL 24. This version of the game included several new game features and upgraded modes, and the Exhaust Engine was used to create more authentic experiences on the ice. Double-check that both the PS5 and PS4 versions are free before claiming them. Lastly, you can gather some friends and wonder which one of them is a horrendous alien that defies mortal comprehension in Among Us. The game became a huge hit during the early days of the COVID-19 pandemic, and since then, it has remained a firm favorite as a social game with its frequent updates and nightmarish April Fools' jokes. If you've never played it, Among Us is all about holding a spaceship together as your crew works to return to civilization. The catch here is that one of your crewmates is a shape-shifting alien, and they'll be aiming to sabotage the ship and kill everyone aboard. The remaining humans have to finish their tasks and identify the Impostors before it's too late. As a bonus in July, PlayStation Plus members can also claim in-game rewards for Genshin Impact with an exclusive bundle.
